Who are we?

We are true crime addicts with a taste for wine! While we sip our curated wines for the evening, we are regailed with stories of mystery, murder, and chaos. The crimes covered in our club range from the seemingly innocent granny who poisons her victims to the serial killer who conned the state of Wisconsin. We always engage with empathy when recounting these cases.
